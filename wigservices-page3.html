✨ Hair Care Tips ✨

  • 🪮 Gently detangle using a wide-tooth comb before washing.
  • 🧴 Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner for longer-lasting wigs.
  • 💨 Air dry wigs instead of blow drying to prevent heat damage.
  • 👩🏽‍🦱 Store wigs on a mannequin or wig stand to maintain shape.
  • 💚 Moisturize the lace and hair strands using light leave-in products.
  • 🧼 Clean your lace front regularly to avoid product buildup.
