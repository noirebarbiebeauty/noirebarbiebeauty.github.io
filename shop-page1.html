💡 Hair Blend 101 – What You Need to Know

A Human Hair Blend wig is a high-quality hybrid crafted by combining real human hair with premium synthetic fibers (either man-made or animal-based). This fusion is designed to deliver the natural feel of human hair with the lightweight styling ease of synthetic strands.

💞 Typical Blend Ratios:
• 50% Human Hair / 50% Synthetic
• 70% Human Hair / 30% Synthetic

✨ Why Choose a Hair Blend?
Hair blend units offer a flawless balance of quality, affordability, and performance. They are:
• More durable and less prone to tangling
• Easier to maintain and style daily
• A budget-conscious alternative to 100% human hair without compromising on the glam

🎀 Understanding the “Blend” Advantage:
1️⃣ A Hair Blend is not just a mix — it’s an engineered enhancement that mimics natural movement and softness while boosting longevity.

2️⃣ Key Benefits:

  • Soft, silky texture with improved manageability
  • Natural-looking hairlines and finish
  • Enhanced color vibrancy and sheen
  • Better structure and styling versatility
